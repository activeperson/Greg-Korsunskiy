About.

I was born in Prague in 1983, and I was fortunate enough to be raised in this beautiful city. Later on, in year 28, I have the privilege of moving to Milan, where I worked in the tourism and fashion industries, as well as an interior designer. In 2011, I moved to Los Angeles and had the amazing experience of visiting about 20 countries in a period of three years.

In 2013, I decided to move back to Prague and dedicate my life to my two passions: art and interior design.

Today, my pieces, my art, is part of several exhibitions around the globe.
